## 🏥 Project: CarePoint Medical Center

A fully responsive, professional website for a local pharmacy and medical clinic — designed to help patients access healthcare services, book appointments online, and build trust in the provider.

---

## 🎯 Business Pitch

### The Problem
Local clinics and pharmacies in Ethiopia often have no digital presence. Patients must physically visit to book appointments, check services, or find contact information — wasting time and reducing accessibility.

### The Solution
CarePoint Medical Center's website solves this by:
- Allowing patients to **book appointments online** in under 2 minutes
- Clearly listing all **services and pricing** so patients come prepared
- Building **trust through testimonials** and professional design
- Providing **instant contact info** and location — reducing unnecessary visits
- Working perfectly on **mobile devices** for patients with smartphones

### Business Impact
| Before Website | After Website |
|---|---|
| Phone-only booking | Online 24/7 booking |
| No service info online | Full service listings |
| No patient reviews visible | Testimonial section |
| Hard to find location | Contact + Google Maps link |
| No professional image | Modern trusted brand |

---

## ✨ Features
- 🦸 **Hero Section** — compelling headline, doctor card, live stats
- 🏥 **About Section** — mission, team highlights, 14+ years badge
- 💊 **6 Services** — General Consultation, Pharmacy, Lab Tests, Specialists, Vaccination, Emergency
- 🖼️ **Gallery** — facility photos with hover overlay
- ⭐ **Testimonials** — 3 real-style patient reviews
- 📅 **Booking Form** — full appointment form with service selection
- 📍 **Contact & Location** — address, phone, hours, map
- ✔ **Trust Bar** — licensed, certified, insured indicators
- 📱 **Fully Responsive** — mobile, tablet, desktop

## 🎨 Design Decisions
- **Font:** DM Serif Display (headings) + Outfit (body) — professional yet approachable
- **Color:** Deep medical blue `#0369a1` — conveys trust and cleanliness
- **Theme:** Clean Medical + Soft Friendly — not cold hospital, not warm café
- **Animations:** Subtle scroll fade-ins — professional feel, not distracting
